Reference Gallery — go look, pull screenshots

The five pillars, sourced.

Open these, screenshot the frames that hit, drop them next to the master prompt in Stitch / Designer. Each card says exactly what to steal — and what to leave.

Pillar 1 · Pop confidence

GTA V — Stephen Bliss art

Cel-shaded, thick-ink, oversaturated, 1970s-movie-poster + pop-art / neo-noir. The swagger.

STEAL: bold composition, confident color blocking, poster framing. LEAVE: the crime/grit, the black outlines on everything.
GTA loading-screen art style explained Loading-screen art gallery (GTA5-Mods) Pinterest · GTA V loading-screen art
Pillar 2 · Handmade soul

Risograph print

Coral + navy ink on warm paper, halftone, slight misregister, grain. Bold forms, reduced palette, real texture.

STEAL: warm-paper ground, duotone, grain, halftone shadows. LEAVE: muddy over-texture, neon riso pinks.
People of Print · 15 riso projects Pinterest · warm risograph posters Dribbble · intro to risograph
Pillar 3 · Sacred geometry

Islamic pattern / Zellij

6- & 8-point tessellation, glazed-terracotta zellij. Clean modern grids, low-relief, never cluttered.

STEAL: lattice as dividers/margins, star motifs, warm tile color. LEAVE: busy full-bleed wallpaper, gold-on-gold kitsch.
Dribbble · modern Islamic pattern Wikipedia · Islamic geometric patterns Pinterest · Islamic design elements
Pillar 4 · Mid-century print

Vintage Cairo travel posters

Art-deco Egypt: minarets, pyramids, retro planes, starlit warm skies, halftone finish, playful minimalist line.

STEAL: heroic warm illustration, limited ink, confident type lockups. LEAVE: literal touristy clichés.
Anderson Design Group · Egypt/Cairo MyRetroposter · Egypt (halftone finish) Pinterest · Egypt vintage posters
Pillar 5 · The craft bar

Editorial app UI — bold & warm

Magazine-grade mobile UI: big type, generous space, left-aligned, poster-cards. The execution quality to match.

STEAL: type scale & rhythm, whitespace, editorial layout. LEAVE: cold minimalism, gray SaaS, hearts-everywhere couple kitsch.
Dribbble · editorial UI Dribbble · bold typography Dribbble · apps for couples (curate hard)
Bonus · Light & mood

Golden-hour photography

Cairo rooftops, warm summer light, long soft shadows — the literal lighting model of the UI. Treat photos in duotone.

STEAL: color temperature, haze, low warm sun. LEAVE: cold blue-hour, harsh flash.

Illustration System — riso scenes

Printed scenes, not vector clipart.
