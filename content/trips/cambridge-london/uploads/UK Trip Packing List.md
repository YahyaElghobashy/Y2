## ⚠️ Before You Pack: Key Context

- **Arriving into a HEATWAVE** (32-36°C first 2-3 days), then cooling to normal English summer (18-22°C days, 12-14°C evenings)
- **It WILL rain** at least once. Probably several times. Pack accordingly.
- **One formal dinner** (Wed July 1, Jesus College) — formal attire required, cultural dress welcomed
- **Four business casual dinners** — smart but not stiff
- **Walking-heavy trip** — cobblestones, long days on foot, markets, college courts
- **Return flight allows 2 checked bags** — room for shopping
- **Laundry:** Jesus College may have guest laundry facilities. Ask at check-in. If not, there are laundrettes in Cambridge. Pack for ~5-6 days of clothes and plan one wash mid-trip.
- **UK plugs are Type G** (3-pronged rectangular). Egyptian plugs (Type C/F) won't fit without an adaptor.

# DO NOT PACK

| Item | Why |
|------|-----|
| Heavy winter coat | It's summer. A light jacket + waterproof is enough. |
| Hair dryer | Hotels and college rooms provide them. |
| Towels | Provided at both accommodations. |
| Too many formal outfits | You only need ONE formal outfit each. The rest is business casual or casual. |
| Valuables you'd worry about | Leave expensive watches, jewelry at home. London has pickpockets at markets. |
| Full-size toiletries | Buy shampoo/conditioner at Boots or Superdrug in Cambridge. Save luggage space. |
| Egyptian food in checked bags | UK customs can be strict on food imports. Pre-packaged sealed items are usually fine, but fresh food and meat products are not allowed. |
