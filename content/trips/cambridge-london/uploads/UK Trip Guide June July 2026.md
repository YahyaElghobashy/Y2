## ZONE 1: SOUTH BANK & BOROUGH MARKET
**The food zone. Your #1 London priority.**

*Nearest Tube: London Bridge (Northern/Jubilee)*

### Borough Market

The famous food market you've been dreaming about. Over 100 stalls of international street food, artisan produce, specialty ingredients, and incredible prepared dishes. This is London's oldest market, dating back over 1,000 years.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Address | 8 Southwark St, SE1 1TL |
| Hours | Tue-Fri 10:00-17:00, Sat 09:00-17:00, Sun 10:00-16:00 |
| **Closed Mondays** | Plan accordingly |
| Best time | Weekday morning (10-12) for exploring without crowds |
| Saturday | Full experience but packed from noon onward |
| Cost | Free entry. Budget £15-25 for a proper food crawl |
| Tips | Wear comfortable shoes (cobblestones). Watch for pickpockets at peak times. |

**Must-try stalls and spots:**
- Seafood (oysters, fish) — multiple vendors with fresh catches
- International street food — Thai, Ethiopian, Spanish paella, Turkish
- Artisan cheese and charcuterie
- Fresh pastries and baked goods
- **Monmouth Coffee** — right inside the market. One of London's finest roasters. Get a flat white.

**Halal note:** Not all stalls are halal, but several Middle Eastern and South Asian vendors offer halal options. Look for signs or ask directly — vendors are used to the question.

### What else is in this zone (walking distance)

| Attraction | Walk from Market | Cost | Appeal | Notes |
|-----------|-----------------|------|--------|-------|
| **The Shard** (viewing platform) | 3 min | £32 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Best panoramic view of London. Book online for discount. |
| **Tate Modern** | 10 min | Free | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | World-class modern art. Turbine Hall alone is worth it. |
| **Shakespeare's Globe** | 8 min | £5 standing, £25+ seated | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Open-air Elizabethan theatre. See a show! |
| **Southwark Cathedral** | 2 min | Free | ⭐⭐⭐ | Beautiful Gothic church right next to market |
| **Millennium Bridge** | 10 min | Free | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Walk across to St Paul's. Iconic photo spot. |
| **South Bank walk** | Starts here | Free | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Walk west along Thames to Westminster. Street performers, book stalls, views. |

**Suggested time for this zone:** 4-5 hours (market + walk + 1-2 attractions)

**Ideal day pairing:** Borough Market morning → Tate Modern → walk across Millennium Bridge to St Paul's → continue to Zone 2 (City) or take Tube to Zone 3 (Westminster)
