## Why This Exists

The marketplace sells "Extra Notification Sends" ‚Äî a way for Yahya or Yara to burst past their daily notification limit by spending CoYYns. T701 built the marketplace card, T407 built BuyExtraSendModal with all the quantity-select and limit-update logic, and T302 handles CoYYn spending. Right now, tapping that card in the marketplace does nothing. This task closes that gap: wire the card to open BuyExtraSendModal directly so the full purchase flow works end-to-end.

This is a plumbing task. No new components. No new UI. Just connecting the pipe that already exists.

---

## User Story

As Yahya or Yara, when I tap "Extra Notification Sends" in the marketplace, I want to see a modal where I can pick how many extra sends I want to buy, confirm the CoYYn cost, and complete the purchase ‚Äî so that I can immediately send more notifications today without waiting for tomorrow's reset.

---

## Full UX Flow

1. User opens `/us/marketplace`
2. User taps the "Extra Notification Sends" card
3. **BuyExtraSendModal (T407) opens** with the quantity selector
4. User selects quantity (1‚Äì5 or whatever T407 supports)
5. User confirms ‚Äî T407 calls `spendCoyyns` (T302) and updates `daily_send_limits.bonus_sends_available` (T402)
6. Modal closes on success
7. User is back on the marketplace page with updated CoYYn balance visible
