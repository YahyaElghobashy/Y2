## Why This Exists

This is the front door. The first screen. The thing Yahya sees when he unlocks his phone and taps the Hayah icon. The thing Yara sees when she checks in. The home dashboard sets the emotional tone for the entire app. It should feel like being greeted by name — warm, personal, unhurried. Not a wall of data, not a productivity dashboard. A calm landing pad that says "welcome back" and offers gentle doorways to wherever you want to go.

---

## User Story

As Yahya, when I open Hayah at 11pm, I see "Good night, Yahya" in elegant serif type, with today's date below. Four cards invite me into our shared modules — Us, Health, Spirit, Ops. Below the cards, placeholder widgets show where future live data will appear (CoYYns balance, cycle tracker, etc.). Everything fades in softly. The whole page breathes. I feel like I've opened a beautiful personal journal, not a to-do app.

---

## Full UX Flow

1. **App opens → Home loads**
2. **Greeting area:**
   - Time-aware greeting in Playfair Display: "Good morning/afternoon/evening/night, Yahya"
   - Below: today's date formatted as "Monday, March 2" in DM Sans
   - Greeting logic:
     - Before 12pm → "Good morning"
     - 12pm–5pm → "Good afternoon"
     - 5pm–9pm → "Good evening"
     - After 9pm → "Good night"
   - Name is hardcoded as "Yahya" for now (becomes dynamic after Auth in Phase 2)
3. **Quick action grid (2×2):**
   - Four QuickActionCard components in a `grid grid-cols-2 gap-4`
   - Cards: Us (Heart), Health (Activity), Spirit (Sun), Ops (CheckSquare)
   - Each card has icon in warm circle, label, description, links to module
4. **Widget slots (below grid):**
   - Two placeholder rectangles where future live widgets will go
   - Each: white card, rounded corners, soft shadow, 100px height
   - Centered muted text: "Widget coming soon"
   - These will later hold: CoYYns wallet balance (Phase 3), cycle day indicator (Phase 5), etc.
5. **Everything wrapped in PageTransition** for the soft fade-in entrance

## Design Details

- No PageHeader on Home — the HomeGreeting IS the header
- No settings gear icon on Home for now (future: add to top-right corner)
- The page has no bottom action button — it's purely navigation and overview
- Widget slots are deliberately plain — they're slots waiting to be filled, not decorative
- Total vertical flow: Greeting → Grid → Widgets → Bottom padding (for nav clearance)

---

## Edge Cases

- Midnight edge case: at exactly 12:00am, greeting should be "Good night" (not "Good morning" — who opens their journal at midnight and wants morning energy?)
- Actually: 12am–5am → "Good night", 5am–12pm → "Good morning". Adjust the logic.
- Date formatting: ensure `date-fns` is imported, not `toLocaleDateString` (consistency)
- Name will become dynamic in Phase 2 — the `name` prop makes this easy to swap later
