## Why This Exists

Daily ops is the practical backbone — grocery lists, task lists, movie watchlists, restaurant wishlists, budget tracking. The stuff that makes shared life run. Not romantic, not spiritual — just functional. But functional in Hayah still means beautiful. A grocery list in this app should feel like writing in a Moleskine, not a spreadsheet. The shell sets that expectation.

---

## User Story

As Yahya or Yara, when I tap "Ops" in the bottom nav, I see the space where our daily shared logistics will live. For now it's empty but the copy hints at shared lists, tasks, and organization tools. It feels like opening a fresh planner.
